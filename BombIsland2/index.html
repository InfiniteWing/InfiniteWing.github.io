ABOUT

Bomb Island II will take you to revisit classic bomberman

Bomb Island II is a game that shows respect for the classical bomberman. In Bomb Island II, player can versus with artifature intellegence(AI). Or have a realtime battle with other player via bluetooth. You will play a bomberman in the game. You can act like a strategist, put the bomb on the path that enemies might pass to destroy them. You can also act like a hero, fight with enemies bravely.

In addition, don't forget your bomb have the chain reaction feature when you were outnumbered. Try on this feature to make a huge chain explosion. You will able to destroy your enemies by surprise.

Bomb Island II uses the online CC material. It is easy to play. Bomb Island II will take you to revisit the classical bomberman.
